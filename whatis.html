What is Urban Exploration?

ur·ban ex·plo·ra·tion (ər-bən ek-splə-ˈrā-shən)

The activity or pastime of exploring man-made structures, especially abandoned buildings and areas not generally open to the public.

"Though urban exploration is super duper illegal, the thrill of experiencing abandoned and derelict environments will have you throwing all caution to the wind!"
